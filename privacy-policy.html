Countdown To Change

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 June 2025  ·  Effective immediately

Countdown To Change ("the app", "we", "us") is a fitness tracking application. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights around it. We keep data collection to what is strictly needed to make the app work.

1. Information we collect

Account information

When you create an account we store your email address and a display name via Firebase Authentication. If you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive only your name and email from those providers — no passwords are ever stored by us.

Profile & fitness data

To personalise your workout plan and nutrition targets we ask for and store: height, weight, goal weight, age, sex, activity level, and fitness goal (cut / bulk / maintain). This is stored in Firebase Firestore under your account.

Workout & training data

Sets, reps, weights, rest times, personal bests, completed workout days, and training streaks are saved locally on your device and synced to Firestore so your data persists across devices.

Progress photos

If you choose to upload progress photos, they are stored in Firebase Storage and are private to your account by default. Photos are only shared with a coach if you explicitly enable that setting.

Health & activity data (optional)

On iOS the app can read step count, calories burned, heart rate, and active energy from Apple Health. On Android it can read equivalent data from Google Health Connect. This permission is optional — the app works fully without it. Health data is displayed in the app but is not uploaded to our servers.

2. How we use your data

  • To create and maintain your account
  • To generate a personalised workout programme and nutrition targets
  • To track your progress, streaks, and milestones over time
  • To send workout reminder notifications (only if you enable them)
  • To restore your data if you reinstall the app or switch devices

We do not sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it for any purpose other than operating the app.

4. Data storage & security

Your data is stored on Firebase servers (Google Cloud infrastructure) located in the EU or US depending on your region. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. Local data on your device is stored using standard platform secure storage.

5. Data retention & deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

6. Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent for optional data collection (e.g. health data)

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. We will update the "last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
